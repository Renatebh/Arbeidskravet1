Who is Mindful Tourism?

Victory awaits him, who has everything
in order - luck we call it.
Defeat is definitely due for him, who has neglected
to take the necessary precautions - bad luck we call it.
-Roald Amundsen

Our founders, Ulf Bjørn, Sjur Mørdre, Lars Hansen and Martin Gudbrand combined two companies to create Mindful Tourism as we know it today. Founded by passionate, exploratory, and unique characters, we hope to pass on their energy and spark into Mindful Tourism clients today. Mindful Tourism strives to show travelers the world of adventure off the beaten path. We want to encourage people to challenge themselves, get out of their comfort zones, and try something brilliant and new.

Norway is an incredible country to explore: learning about our nature, sustainable infrastructure, Norwegian culture and philosophies such as Friluftsliv and Dugnad. Not only will our trips broaden your horizons intellectually, we also hope to broaden your personal perspectives, by showing you the world we know and love: the vast natural landscapes of Norway.
As a local tour operator, giving back to our communities is a paramount part of our culture, and we strive to conserve the environments we work in. Part of the Mindful Tourism experience is to educate travelers on how to explore thoughtfully, learn about local conservation, and consider impacts on the environments we’re observing. Mindful Tourism created The Caring Foundation and is partnered with the Adventure Tourism Conservation Fund,

Responsible Travel, and The Adventure Travel Trade Association.

On trips with Mindful Tourism, our number one priority is, simply, you. Safety is on top of the list at Norrøna Mindful Tourism when considering joining our adventures. The backbone of Mindful Tourism is our guides. Finding guides with the right diploma, who held the right courses and right practical experience is a demanding task.
However, for Mindful Tourism the soft – social skills, are as important as the hard ­­– practical skills. With their invaluable knowledge, our guides make us proud, and we are proud to have them representing us. We hire guides that are competent, experienced, English-speaking, and first-aid trained. With their care for the environment, love of nature, open minds and knowledge of the environment, we are confident our guides will give you a fulfilling and adventurous experience.

Last, but most important, is our fundamental love for nature, culture, traveling, and adventure. Join a trip with us to experience Norway for yourself!

After all, no one knows Norway like us.
