Green things to do

Autumn in Norway is something everyone should experience. Escape the big crowds and the hustle and bustle, and enjoy our country as the landscape get painted in red, yellow, and orange. Even though the weather is wetter and colder, it’s relaxing to be a part of the picturesque surroundings during this season. People of all ages head outdoors to enjoy activities in the mountains and forests, along the coast, or in the cities. And the best of all – many of these activities are environmentally friendly with little or no impact on local ecosystems.
